FAQ

How Amadeus Works ?

Amadeus is an intelligent AI based chrome extension making use of Flask webapp as our backend running on python-based servers.

Did we use API's ?

Obviously, such a product rests on the back of some powerful API's such as Meaning Cloud, Chuck Norris Database, Google Cloud Vision, Firebase Hosting, DialogFlow, Google Cloud Server, Google Maps, Google Weather, OpenCV etc.

Why name it Amadeus?

Amadeus was a powerful AI assistant in one of our favourite anime "Steins;Gate 0". So, owing to our love towards this show, we named it Amadeus.
