About Us

We are a diverse group of over-enthusiastic robotics nerds from all the departments of college. The club is a student body which runs under the umbrella of Student Activity Centre MNNIT ALLAHABAD. We build robots for academic purposes, to compete at national events or just out of freaky minds. Fascinated but Clueless? No worry. We are here to induct you to the rapidly expanding world of robotics. One on one guidance, workshops and tutorials along with tools, equipments, components and workspace eagerly awaits you!
