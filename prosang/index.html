ABOUT US

For the first time in its history, MNNIT-Allahabad is going to organise a TECHNO SUMMIT in the second week of April to showcase the knowledge imbibed upon all throughout the year. Apart from the annual technical fest of the college - Avishkar, this event is the brainchild of the Robotics, Aero-modelling, Energy Club, Electronics Society and other Technical clubs.
So get ready to witness talk shows from experienced and renowned technical experts, innovative exhibitions, air show, fascinating workshops and the most challenging inter-college and inter-school competitions.

Robotics Club

We are a diverse group of over-enthusiastic robotics nerds from all the departments of college. The club is a student body which runs under the umbrella of Student Activity Centre MNNIT ALLAHABAD.We build robots for academic purposes, to compete at national events or just out of freaky minds. Fascinated but Clueless? No worry. We are here to induct you to the rapidly expanding world of robotics. One on one guidance, workshops and tutorials along with tools, equipments, components and workspace eagerly awaits you!

Aeromodelling Club

Our love for aircraft, fervour to fly and perseverance, has been able to uphold the legacy of excellence and made club stand this tall. The club is a student body comprising of aerospace enthusiasts which was founded in 2009 and since then the club has come afar. We build aerial vehicles for academic purposes, to compete at national events or just out of freaky minds. Till now our mission was to acquire knowledge about aerial systems through experiments with models and knowledge transfer to its members and now we look forward to sharing our knowledge beyond college as well. We have also initiated some work on Astronomy, Astrophysics, Astrobiology, Space Science and Propulsion System. Also occasionally we arrange stargazing and related events.

FAQ

How Can I Reach Allahabad?

Allahabad is well connected to major cities like Lucknow, Kanpur, Varanasi and Delhi via Airways, Railways and Roadways.

  • Roadways
  • Railways
  • Airways
  • Ola & Uber

Do we provide travel cost?

We can’t guarantee reimbursements for everyone, but we don’t want money to keep you from experiencing ProSang. You’ll get a reimbursement amount along with your invitation, in case of some special case. Let us know if you still need help making your way to Allahabad and we’ll see what we can do.

Can I start working on my project before the event?

No. In the interest of fairness, students should not be working on their projects before ProSang begins and we do not allow participants to work on pre-existing projects. However, you can familiarize yourself with all the tools and technologies you intend to use beforehand!

What is the registration fee and mode of payment?

Registration fee is 600 INR, which includes participation to all events, talk shows, exhibitions and air shows. Besides, it also includes a t-shirt each for the participant. Mode of payment is online via UPI code provided during the registration process and you have to submit the payment id during confirmation.

Will accommodation be provided?

Yes, accommodation is provided by payment of 200 more with the registration fee inside the campus. You will be reponsible for any damage to the college property.
