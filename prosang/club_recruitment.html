Aero-Robotics Club MNNIT

Hello there,

We are the Aeromodelling and Robotics Club of Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology, Allahabad, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh. We are inviting interested and passionate outside members from different colleges to join us onboard and help us in our different projects.

Introducing, Aeromodelling Club is founded in 2008, by some highly enthusiastic and motivated seniors in the field of Aeromodelling and drones. Currently we have increased our working to automated drones, propulsion system, spy gliders etc. We have also initiated work on astronomy, astrophysics, astrobiology, etc. Some of our project on drones are:

  • 1) Swarm Aerial Bots
  • 2) Gesture Controlled Bots
  • 3) Carrier Drones (Completely controlled over 4G mobile network and controlled via an mobile app from anywhere round the world)
  • 4) Spy gliders (Automated and live stream capability, mainly focussed to serve with the military)

Introducing Robotics Club, founded in 2016, issue projects every year. This year we are working on multiple projects of high interest in advanced robotics and prosthesis. Some of the projects are:

  • 1) Humanoid Bot
  • 2) Self Driving Bot
  • 3) Bionic Arm
  • 4) Brain mapping and stress sensing
  • 5) Automatic Irrigation
  • 6) Smart Watch
  • 7) Jarvis

All the projects are meant to be commercialised finally, hence also giving the student a practical aspect to the complete process from ideation, designing, prototyping and mass production at industrial level.

Hope we will be successful in our aim to create a better society with better and practical technical knowledge and practical skill to all.
